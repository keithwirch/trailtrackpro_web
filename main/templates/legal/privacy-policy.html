PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

LEGAL SUMMARY: TrailTrack Pro is designed with privacy in mind. Your photos, videos, and property data are stored locally on your devices and are never uploaded to our servers. We only collect minimal information necessary to validate your software license.

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy explains how Wirchbench Networks ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the TrailTrack Pro desktop application and the TrailTrack Companion mobile app (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. INFORMATION COLLECTION

2.1 License Activation Data

When you activate a software license, we collect the following limited information:

  • LICENSE KEY: The unique identifier used to activate your software.
  • MACHINE ID: A non-personally identifiable hashed string derived from your computer's hostname.
  • VERSION DATA: The version of TrailTrack Pro installed and your operating system type.

NOTE: We do NOT collect your Internet Protocol (IP) address during license activation or subsequent validations.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

The following data remains strictly private and is never collected or viewed by us:

  • Your media files (photos, videos, thumbnails).
  • Property names, addresses, and boundaries.
  • Global Positioning System (GPS) coordinates for camera locations.
  • User analytics or behavioral tracking data.

2.3 Local Data Storage

All application data, including your media library and mapping metadata, is stored exclusively on your local device. Transmission of this data only occurs between your own devices over your private local network.

3. MOBILE COMPANION APP

The TrailTrack Companion mobile app facilitates direct file transfer to your desktop computer via a local WiFi connection. Files shared through the mobile app are transferred directly to your computer and are never routed through our infrastructure. The app may request GPS permissions solely to mark coordinates for transmission to your local desktop database.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

4.1 Map Imagery

Satellite mapping services are provided by Esri ArcGIS World Imagery. When maps are displayed, your device makes direct requests to Esri's servers. These interactions are subject to Esri's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Software Updates

Version checks and update downloads are facilitated via GitHub. This connectivity is subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement.

5. DATA USE AND RETENTION

Collected license data is used solely for the purpose of valid licensing and support. This data is retained for the duration of your license term. You may request deletion of license-associated data by deactivating your license key through the software settings.

6. SECURITY

We employ standard encryption protocols (HTTPS) for all license-related communications. However, since the bulk of your data is stored locally, the primary security of your data depends on the security of your own devices and networks.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. JURISDICTION

Wirchbench Networks operates under the laws of the State of Wisconsin, USA. License data is processed and stored within the United States.
